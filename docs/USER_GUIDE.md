## 1. Getting Started
### Logging In
Access the portal at [https://darpan.yuktaa.com](https://darpan.yuktaa.com). Enter your registered email and password.

### Profile Management
Click on your avatar in the top right corner to view your profile, change your password, or log out.

## 2. Dashboard
The Dashboard is your central hub. It displays:
*   **Quick Stats**: Pending approvals, active tasks, etc.
*   **Shortcuts**: Quick links to frequently used modules.
*   **Announcements**: Important company-wide updates.
