# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** December 5, 2025

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to Darpan ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you access our enterprise work portal.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information
We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the portal, including:
*   Name
*   Email address
*   Phone number
*   Date of Birth / Date of Joining
*   Employee ID
*   Role/Designation

### 2.2 Operational Data
As an enterprise portal, we process business-related data entered by you, such as:
*   Expense reports and receipts
*   Purchase orders and vendor details
*   Task status and completion data
*   Training progress and quiz scores

## 3. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information for the following purposes:
*   **Account Management**: To create and manage your user account.
*   **Business Operations**: To facilitate expense approvals, purchasing workflows, and task management.
*   **Communication**: To send notifications regarding tasks, approvals, or system updates.
*   **Security**: To monitor and prevent unauthorized access.

## 4. Data Security
We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data, including:
*   Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS).
*   Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) to restrict data access.
*   Regular security audits.

## 5. Data Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We may share generic aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal identification information regarding visitors and users with our business partners and trusted affiliates.

## 6. Your Rights
You have the right to:
*   Access your personal data.
*   Request correction of inaccurate data.
*   Request deletion of your data (subject to company retention policies).
